Del Mar Daffy - 1975 Hugh Holland

Hugh Holland is an American photographer best known for his images of skateboarders riding around the empty pools and concrete playgrounds of southern California. His work, shot during the mid-1970s, captures a very specific period of time in the sport when it was unburdened by outside financial interests, yet was also a wildly popular counterculture among teenagers.
